AI SaaS Chatbot — Product FAQ & Knowledge Base
================================================

## What is this product?
This is an AI-powered SaaS chatbot platform built using Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG).
It allows businesses to upload their own documents and instantly get an intelligent chatbot
that answers questions based on that content — accurately, without hallucination.

## How does it work?
1. You upload your documents (PDF, Word, CSV, Excel, TXT, JSON).
2. The system automatically processes and indexes your content.
3. Users ask questions in natural language.
4. The AI retrieves the most relevant parts of your documents.
5. It generates a precise, grounded answer — citing your own content.

## What file types are supported?
Currently supported formats:
- PDF documents
- Plain text files (.txt)
- CSV spreadsheets
- Excel files (.xlsx, .xls)
- JSON files
- Word documents (coming soon)

## What AI models power the chatbot?
The platform uses best-in-class language models:
- Primary: Llama 3.3 70B via Groq (ultra-fast, free tier available)
- Fallback: Llama 3.1 8B Instant (fast, lightweight)
- Optional: Google Gemini 2.0 Flash (for production deployments)

## How fast are responses?
With Groq-powered inference, responses typically arrive in under 1 second.
Average latency is 300–600ms for most queries, even on complex documents.

## Is my data private?
Yes. Your documents are stored locally in a ChromaDB vector database on your own server.
No document content is sent to third parties — only the query and retrieved chunks
are sent to the AI model for generation.

## What are the pricing tiers?
- Free tier: Up to 50 queries/day, 1 document upload, community support.
- Starter ($19/month): 2,000 queries/day, 10 document uploads, email support.
- Pro ($49/month): 10,000 queries/day, unlimited uploads, priority support, custom branding.
- Enterprise: Custom pricing, on-premise deployment, SLA guarantees.

## How do I upload documents?
Use the dashboard to drag and drop files, or use the REST API endpoint POST /ingest
to programmatically upload documents from your own systems.

## Can I customise the chatbot's personality?
Yes. The system prompt is fully configurable. You can set the chatbot's name,
tone (formal, casual, technical), language, and response style from the dashboard.

## What languages does it support?
The chatbot supports all languages that the underlying LLM supports.
This includes English, Spanish, French, German, Arabic, Chinese, Japanese, and more.
For best results with non-English documents, use Google Gemini models.

## How is accuracy ensured?
The RAG architecture grounds all answers in your uploaded content.
The AI is instructed to only answer from provided context and say
"I don't have enough information" when the answer isn't in your documents.
This prevents hallucination and keeps responses accurate.

## What is the API?
The platform exposes a REST API:
- GET  /         — Health check
- POST /ask      — Submit a question, receive an answer with latency stats
- POST /ingest   — Upload new documents (coming soon via API)
- GET  /docs     — Interactive API documentation (Swagger UI)

## How do I get started?
1. Clone the repository or access the hosted version.
2. Add your GROQ_API_KEY to the .env file.
3. Place your documents in the data/ folder.
4. Run: python backend/ingest.py
5. Start the server: python -m uvicorn backend.api:app --port 8000
6. Query at: http://localhost:8000/ask

## What happens if the AI doesn't know the answer?
The chatbot will respond: "I don't have enough information to answer that accurately."
It will never fabricate information. If your documents don't contain the answer,
the bot will say so clearly rather than guessing.

Yes. The API is fully stateless and supports concurrent requests.
Session management and per-user chat history is on the product roadmap.

## What is retrieval-augmented generation (RAG)?
RAG is an AI technique that combines a search system with a language model.
Instead of relying on the model's training data, RAG retrieves relevant content
from your own documents first, then uses the LLM to generate an answer based only
on that retrieved content. This makes the AI accurate, up-to-date, and trustworthy.

## What are embeddings?
Embeddings are numerical vector representations of text.
When you upload a document, it is split into chunks and each chunk is converted
into a vector using a local HuggingFace model (BAAI/bge-small-en-v1.5).
These vectors capture the meaning of the text and enable semantic search —
finding relevant content by meaning, not just keyword matching.
